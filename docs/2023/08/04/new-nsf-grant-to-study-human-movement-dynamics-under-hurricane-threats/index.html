New NSF grant to study human movement dynamics under hurricane threats

Posted by thatdrwho on Friday, August 4, 2023

Dr. Yujie Hu and collaborators Dr. Leo Duan and Dr. George Michailidis have received a new grant from the National Science Foundation to conduct geospatial modeling and risk mitigation for human movement dynamics under hurricane threats.
