Dr. Yujie Hu is awarded the <a href="https://informatics.institute.ufl.edu/ufii-programs/seed-funds/covid-19-response-seed-funding/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">COVID-19 Response SEED Funding</a> from <a href="https://informatics.institute.ufl.edu" target="_blank" rel="noopener">UFII</a> (UF Informatics Institute). This competitive research award provides seed money for applications for data science, machine learning, AI, and mathematical modeling related research that can be rapidly mobilized to help address various facets of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Dr. Hu and his collaborator will be examining the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic on crimes. His team is looking for two student OPS positions (undergrad or grad) to contribute to this project. Refer to <a href="https://geog.ufl.edu/2020/06/17/ops-opportunity-for-socioeconomic-impacts-of-covid-19-a-criminological-perspective/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">this link</a> for more detail about the positions and how to apply.
